Can't Be Beat

Over the past ten years, I've worked in a large variety of restraunts. From server to fry cook, I've seen it all. But most importantly I saw the issue with inventory among smaller restruants. Bigger chains could take the risk of over ordering or throwing out waste, but not the mom and pop shops. I've witnessed countless moment of disgrunteled workers and stressed managers over supply issues: "half the pantry has to be 86", "we're too far over budget to order more this week", etc. I'm hoping my product can reduce those moments for everyone involved.
